Rabies vaccine

  • Dose: 5ml for children and adults
  • Pre-exposure vaccination:
    • Three doses of 0.5 ml of VERORAB® are administered at D0, D7, and D28 for primary vaccination.
    • The dose scheduled at D28 can be administered at D21.
  • Post-exposure vaccination:
    • Zagreb Regimen: four doses of 0.5 ml are administered: one dose in the right deltoid region and one dose in the left deltoid region at D0, then one dose is administered in the deltoid region at D7 and D21.
  • Rabies immunoglobulins should be administered at D0 concomitantly with the vaccine, in case of category III
    • exposure (WHO classification, Single or multiple transdermal bites or scratches Licks on broken skin
    • Contamination of mucous membrane with saliva (i.e., licks) Exposure to bats).
  • The rabies immunoglobulins Dosology is as follows:
    • Human rabies immunoglobulins 20 IU/kg of body weight,
    • Equine rabies immunoglobulins 40 IU/kg of body weight
  • For more information, please see the package leaflet of the rabies immunoglobulins used.
  • When possible, the vaccine should be administered contra-laterally to the immunoglobulins administration sites.
